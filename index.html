About

Editor’s instinct. Director’s control.

I’m Aidan Serik, a film editor and director based in Tbilisi and Almaty, working worldwide. My background spans feature films, documentaries, interviews, shorts, and public-facing trailer work. I’m drawn to material that needs sensitivity, shape, and cuts that feel inevitable rather than decorative.

The idea behind this site is simple: to present work the way final credits feel — spare, deliberate, and memorable. Less noise. More authorship.
