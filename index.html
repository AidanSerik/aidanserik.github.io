About

A feature-first editor with a director’s eye.

I’m Aidan Serik, a film editor and director based in Tbilisi and Almaty, working worldwide. My background moves across feature films, short films, documentary series, interviews, branded storytelling, and direction. What ties everything together is an instinct for rhythm, emotional logic, and cuts that feel inevitable rather than decorative.

I’m especially interested in projects that need sensitivity, structure, and cinematic restraint — work where editorial choices shape the story rather than simply accelerate it.
