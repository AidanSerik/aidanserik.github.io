About

A cleaner, softer, more stylish visual language.

I’m Aidan Serik, a film editor and director based in Tbilisi and Almaty, working worldwide. My background spans feature films, documentaries, interviews, short films, and trailer work. I’m especially drawn to projects that need sensitivity, structure, and editorial choices that shape the story rather than simply accelerate it.

This design direction stays minimal, but not flat. The goal is a portfolio that feels current, composed, and visually confident.
