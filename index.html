About

About the editor.

I’m Aidan Serik, a film editor and director based in Tbilisi and Almaty, working worldwide. My background spans feature films, documentaries, short films, interviews, and trailer work. I’m especially drawn to projects that need sensitivity, structure, and cuts that shape the emotional logic of a story rather than simply speed it up.

The idea behind this site is simple: keep the atmosphere of film titles and final credits, but make the experience practical enough for producers, directors, and premium freelance clients to browse quickly.
